Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Legislative Tracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our application.

2. Data We Collect & How We Use It

We collect and process the following types of data:

  • Identity Data: If you sign in, we store your email address and basic profile information provided by Google Authentication to create your account.
  • Usage Data: We store the lists of bills you mark as "Favorites" to provide dashboard functionality.
  • Address & Location Data (Transient): You may optionally provide your address to identify your specific legislative districts & representatives. We do not store this data. Your address is sent to our geocoding service solely to retrieve your district information and is discarded immediately thereafter. Neither your street address nor your geographic coordinates are saved in our database.

3. Data Retention

We only retain your personal data (Identity and Favorites) for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. As stated above, address data is never retained.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Firebase for authentication and hosting, and OpenStates for legislative data. Please review the Google Privacy Policy and the OpenStates Terms of Service & Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the "Report a Bug" link in the footer.
